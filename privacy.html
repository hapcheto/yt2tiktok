Privacy Policy – yt2tiktok

Effective date: January 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how yt2tiktok (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use the yt2tiktok app/service (“Service”).

What the Service does

yt2tiktok helps users publish videos to TikTok using TikTok’s official APIs (e.g., Content Posting API). It may fetch publicly available YouTube video URLs and generate clips for posting, depending on your setup.

Information we collect

  • TikTok authorization data: OAuth authorization code/access token/refresh token, TikTok user identifier, and granted scopes.
  • Content & metadata you provide: video files you upload/generate, captions, hashtags, cover selection, scheduling info.
  • Technical/log data: timestamps, request IDs, error logs, IP address (if hosted as a web service), and basic device/browser info (if applicable).

How we use information

  • To authenticate you with TikTok and perform actions you request (upload/publish videos).
  • To operate, secure, and improve the Service (debugging, fraud prevention, reliability).
  • To provide support (if you contact us).

Legal basis (EEA/UK, if applicable)

We process data to perform the contract with you (provide the Service), to comply with legal obligations, and for legitimate interests (security and service improvement).

Sharing of information

  • TikTok: We send content and required data to TikTok to publish videos via TikTok APIs.
  • Service providers: hosting/storage/logging providers only as needed to run the Service.
  • Legal: if required by law or to protect rights/safety.

We do not sell your personal data.

Data retention

We retain TikTok tokens and operational logs only as long as necessary to provide the Service and for security. You can revoke access in TikTok settings or request deletion by contacting us.

Security
